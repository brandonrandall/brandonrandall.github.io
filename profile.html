The hardest thing about mod 1

I would say the hardest thing about mod 1 was adjusting my mindset into that of a programmer. My experience with mod 1 was different than most as I started with zero experience. I had to also learn the basic shortcuts on a Macbook along with learning to type more than letters. After defeating those little foes, I then had to learn basic ruby syntax and how to create collections and iterate through them. Although those things were difficult, I still have enjoyed this process and wont stop until I am a true software developer.
